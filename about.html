Who I am

I am an aspiring 3D Artist from Campbell River, British Columbia, Canada.
I was first introduced to the world of 3D at the beginning of high school 5 years ago.
I am now in my first year of study at North Island College in their Advanced Design and Development Diploma program.

In highschool I became proficient with 3D software Blender, completing both personal and educational projects.
Within Blender, most of my time was focused on modeling and rendering projects, however, I also spent a few months flexing my animation muscles.

Most of my works include those from my hobbies, mainly video games and historical warships.
However, I have completed a donut project, lego man project, and attempted my hand at character models. Check out my projects!
