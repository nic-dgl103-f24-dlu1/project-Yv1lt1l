Who Am I?

Hi, I'm an aspiring Canadian 3D artist! I first got into contact with the 3D Industry in High School and have since fallen in love with it. Most of my work has been with still images and inanimate objects, however, I am no stranger to animation work.

More About Me

Why Go With Me?

Locally operating in British Columbia, I have been creating renders for the past 4 years. My works have been featured in my high schools art competitions. I will consult with you in every major step and process of your creation, making sure your model is exactly how you envision. My worklfow makes use of powerful cutting-edge tools to ensure quality meets expectations.
