My Projects

These are some of the projects I've worked on thus far.

Donut Project

This is a project I completed over the course of a month. It was my first ever Blender project.

Yamato Project

This was my first solo project as part of my capstone project, and the one I'm most fond and passionate about. It depicts World War Two Japanese battleship Yamato, the most powerful battleship ever built. This render won artwork of the week against three other classes.

Battleship Nagato Project

My second solo project was the result of a challenge from a peer. It depicts battleship Nagato in its Second World War configuration. She sustained two direct nuclear blasts as part of post war nuclear testing (Operation Crossroads), only to sink due to a torpedo hit several weeks before.
